# Literature references

The PDFs of the foundational papers for this project were kept locally during development but are not redistributed in this repository. The citations below identify every source used in `docs/lit_review.md` and throughout the library documentation.

1. Billera, L. J., Holmes, S. P., and Vogtmann, K. (2001). Geometry of the space of phylogenetic trees. *Advances in Applied Mathematics*, 27(4), 733 to 767. DOI: 10.1006/aama.2001.0759

2. Owen, M. and Provan, J. S. (2011). A fast algorithm for computing geodesic distances in tree space. *IEEE/ACM Transactions on Computational Biology and Bioinformatics*, 8(1), 2 to 13. DOI: 10.1109/TCBB.2010.3

3. Miller, E., Owen, M., and Provan, J. S. (2015). Polyhedral computational geometry for averaging metric phylogenetic trees. *Advances in Applied Mathematics*, 68, 51 to 91. DOI: 10.1016/j.aam.2015.04.002

4. Brown, D. G. and Owen, M. (2020). Mean and variance of phylogenetic trees. *Systematic Biology*, 69(1), 139 to 154. DOI: 10.1093/sysbio/syz041

5. Bacak, M. (2014). Computing medians and means in Hadamard spaces. *SIAM Journal on Optimization*, 24(3), 1542 to 1566. DOI: 10.1137/140953393

6. Lin, B., Sturmfels, B., Tang, X., and Yoshida, R. (2017). Convexity in tree spaces. *SIAM Journal on Discrete Mathematics*, 31(3), 2015 to 2038. DOI: 10.1137/16M1079841

7. Moon, J. W. (1970). *Counting Labelled Trees*. Canadian Mathematical Monographs 1. Canadian Mathematical Congress.

8. Robinson, D. F. and Foulds, L. R. (1981). Comparison of phylogenetic trees. *Mathematical Biosciences*, 53(1 to 2), 131 to 147. DOI: 10.1016/0025 5564(81)90043 2

9. Bryant, D. and Moulton, V. (2004). Neighbor Net: an agglomerative method for the construction of phylogenetic networks. *Molecular Biology and Evolution*, 21(2), 255 to 265. DOI: 10.1093/molbev/msh018

10. Allman, E. S. and Rhodes, J. A. Lectures on phylogenetics from an algebraic viewpoint. Lecture notes.

11. Connecting BHV spaces of different taxa. Reference used for Section on extending tree space across non identical leaf sets in `docs/lit_review.md`.

12. Tree rearrangement and Robinson Foulds. Reference used for the NNI rearrangement graph section.

13. Uniqueness and intractability of phylogenetic networks. Reference used for the networks section.
